About our Membership

As an unregistered user of our website, you will only be able to see the handful of weekly featured puzzles posted by us. Any user-submitted puzzles become available only upon signing up, and fortunately for you, this is easy! You can choose to be a member for free with a limited daily number of user puzzles at your disposal, or you can increase this number with a small monthly fee.

We are also eagerly looking for volunteers to serve as website moderators so we can maintain order, efficiency, and available assistance in our community. Moderators have the responsibility of handling user submissions to ensure that they are solvable and meet our standards. Additionally, they serve as guides for any questions users may have and oversee any policy violations.
